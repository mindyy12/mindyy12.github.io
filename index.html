Chewy — end to end product In progress

Most nutrition apps fail the consistency test — too many taps, too much friction for something you're supposed to do every day. Chewy is a rethink inspired by the UX simplicity of Hevy: a chat-first calorie tracker where you describe your meal in plain language and it logs it instantly. The same interface doubles as a recipe builder, with a profile view for daily intake and goal history. Built end-to-end solo to develop real product thinking across design, dev, and UX.
